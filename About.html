History of SKY NDT

Endorsed as a startup by Ajinkya Navale & Manoj Badgujar, Sky ndtService was founded with a distinct mission. We are the only firm inIndia leveraging pioneering strategies to uplift the standard ofindigenous engineering services. Incepted in the industrial stronghold ofGujarat in 2022. SKY NDT embarked on its path by offering unparalleledNon-Destructive Testing (NDT) services to a multitude of sec tors such ascross-country pipelines, refineries, power plants, aerospace etc.

The brainchild of an ambitious group of first-generation entrepreneurs, SKY NDTsteadfastly fulfils its commitment to supply efficient, innovative NDT solutions, ensuringall projects are brought to completion within their stipulated timelines. Ourcomprehensive NDT services include Ultrasonic Testing and Thickness Testing, MagneticParticle and Liquid Penetrant Inspection, PMI, Hardness by portable Hardness Tester, DiePenetrant test among others.
